Our Story

40 Years of Stargazing

Founded in 1985 by a group of passionate astronomers and educators, the CosmicDome started as a small projector in a community center. Today, we are one of the region's premier science institutions.

In 2015, we completed a major renovation, installing our signature 60-foot dome and an 8K digital projection system that allows us to simulate the universe with unprecedented accuracy.

"Our goal is to make the cosmos accessible to everyone, regardless of age or background."
