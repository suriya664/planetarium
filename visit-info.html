Getting Here

By Car

Take Exit 42 from I-95. Follow signs for "Museum District". We offer a free parking lot with 200 spaces, including accessible spots close to the entrance.

Public Transit

Bus routes #5 and #12 stop directly in front of the Planetarium. The "Space Center" Metro station is a 10-minute walk (0.5 mi).

By Bike

Bike racks are located near the main entrance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Planetarium wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our entire facility including the dome theater, exhibits, and restrooms are fully wheelchair accessible. We also have designated seating in the dome.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

Yes, tickets are sold at our box office, but we strongly recommend booking online in advance as shows often sell out, especially on weekends.

Is photography allowed?

Photography is allowed in the exhibit areas. However, inside the dome theater during shows, ALL photography and glowing screens are strictly prohibited as they ruin the dark adaptation for other guests.
